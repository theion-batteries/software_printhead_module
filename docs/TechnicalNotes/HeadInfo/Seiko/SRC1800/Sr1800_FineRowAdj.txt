Reg Nibble


SRC1800 head is made of 4 modules (nozzle rows) M_A, M_B, M_C, M_D (see the picture above)
Their offsets within the head are: 0, 137, 222, 359 pix.
Each HDC drives 2 heads, so, there are 8 HDC nozzle rows, and they are interleaved.

Every HDC nozzle row has its own nibble (4 bits representing offsets in 1/16 of a pixel) in a tick offset register.
Thus, there are 8 nibbles in forward and 8 nibbles in reverse tick offset registers.

As mentioned in a default config file, each nozzle row (head module) can be individually 
adjusted by using 4 values, like:

[HeadX:Y:Z]
Row_X_Adj_cpix  = val0,  val1,  val2,  val3

For head orientation 0 val0 corresponds to the M_A nozzle row adjust,  val1->M_B,  val2->M_C,  val3->M2_D
For head orientation 1 val0 corresponds to the M_D nozzle row adjust,  val1->M_C,  val2->M_B,  val3->MA
